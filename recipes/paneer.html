Paneer Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g Paneer
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the paneer cubes and gently stir to coat them with the masala.
  8. Add cream or yogurt and mix well. Cook for another 5 minutes on low heat.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
